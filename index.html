Mental Health Problems Get Worse

Teenagers already face many emotional challenges

The teenage brain is still learning to handle emotions and stress

Alcohol stops this natural learning process

  • Heavy-drinking teenagers show more depression and anxiety
  • Binge drinking (4-5 drinks in one night) is especially harmful
  • Girls and boys are affected differently - but both are harmed
  • Even after stopping drinking, mental health problems continue
